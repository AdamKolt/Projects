About Me


I build the systems that make sellers and partners productive.

Across Telecom, Government, and IT, I’ve spent 20+ years designing and running the academies, certifications, playbooks, and content systems that turn GTM strategy into repeatable execution — from onboarding new reps to enabling full channel partner ecosystems.

At Ooredoo I built and ran the B2B Sales Academy — a two-tier Sales Associate and Sales Expert certification program serving 200+ employees and channel partners across Mobile, Fixed, and ICT lines. The academy achieved 85% certification completion and delivered a 15% post-certification performance uplift, while cutting frontline ramp time from four months to two.

Alongside the academy, I led the SOHO indirect channel partners program — 5 partner firms with ~125 agents delivering both sales and retention independently — enabled by a connected-tablet stack (CMS, CRM, Field App), standardized playbooks, and co-sell plays with hyperscalers like Microsoft.

What differentiates me today is the AI-native layer. I use GPT-4o to generate discovery question banks, objection libraries, use-case cheat sheets, competitive teardowns, and MCQ certification quizzes — compressing content production from weeks to days. Paired with a RAG-based knowledge assistant in the seller workflow, it’s a modern enablement stack that keeps reps and partners current without expanding headcount.

The business and technical depth — an MBA, a B.E.E., and formal program management training — lets me move fluidly from boardroom narrative to instructional design to systems architecture in the same day. That’s what enablement at scale actually requires.
