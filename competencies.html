The Enablement Operating System

I treat enablement as a system, not a set of deliverables. Three layers — Programs, Content & Delivery, and AI & Analytics — working together to produce one outcome: productive sellers and productive partners.

1 Programs — Academies, Certifications & Playbooks

  • Designs tiered certification academies (Sales Associate → Sales Expert) anchored to real sales stages — B2B Sales Academy certified 200+ sellers and partners with 85% completion and 15% post-cert uplift.
  • Builds onboarding and ramp programs that cut frontline ramp time from four months to two, with learning paths tied to pipeline stage and role.
  • Codifies the sales motion into playbooks — discovery scripts, qualification criteria, and objection libraries — so reps run a repeatable play instead of improvising.
  • Owns SKO planning, QBRs, and win/loss programs as recurring rhythms that keep the field calibrated to strategy, not just informed about it.

2 Content & Delivery — CMS, ILT, and Blended Learning

  • Stands up centralized sales CMS platforms as the single source of truth for collateral, pricing, battlecards, and training — with governance that keeps content current as products, pricing, and competitors change.
  • Orchestrates blended delivery: instructor-led cohorts, recorded on-demand modules, periodic boot camps, and weekly performance reviews — run a bench of 15 product-manager instructors plus SMEs.
  • Extends the same academy standard to channel partners — the SOHO indirect channel program (5 firms, ~125 agents) ran the same certification path as direct sellers, enabling a consistent sales-and-retention motion across channels.
  • Equips the field with connected-tablet stacks (CMS + mobile CRM + field app) so collateral, customer context, and order processing live in one in-workflow surface.
  • Modernizes order forms into fillable, digital documents with e-signature workflows — eliminating paper handoffs and compressing order cycle time from days to hours.

3 AI & Analytics — Modern Enablement Stack

  • Runs an AI-native content factory on Copilot and Claude — generating discovery question banks, objection-and-response libraries, use-case cheat sheets, competitive teardowns, and MCQ certification quizzes. Content cycles compressed from weeks to days.
  • Deploys RAG-based knowledge assistants integrated to CRM and tariff libraries, so reps and partners query live context in-workflow rather than hunting through drives.
  • Builds enablement KPIs including certification completion, ramp time, post-cert performance uplift, win rate, quota attainment, sales cycle length , and content consumption — tied back to pipeline and performance.
  • Instruments executive reporting (Power BI, Excel) that rolls enablement metrics into the same revenue dashboards leadership already reads.
